TAJ Project

The TAJ project is a game developed to the NAVE integrated workshop. It is happening in India and show the life of a Indian school driver with a mission: find the girl's toy and take her to the school.

Insighting - Desing Thinking

Insighting is an app where the user can transform their research findings into insight cards, find patterns between them and then categorize through mobile, more agile..

Papin

The Papin is an educational game for kids and it was made in my first year as a programmer. The game consists in finding paleolithic's objects in the scenario and learn about them. This project is simple, but it is great fun.

Eld - A new way of cherishing elders

A sensor that checks your blood pressure and heart rate, and sends data to an app that will create daily charts in order to send them to your doctor, family or even the police. It can also help avoid some diseases.
